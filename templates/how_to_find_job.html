How to Find a Job in 7 Steps

A concise guide by Jobify.ro to help you land your next role faster.

1. Define Your Goal

Clarify your target role, industry, salary expectations and preferred locations.

2. Polish Your CV

Tailor your resume for each vacancy. Use our CV Builder to generate a polished PDF.

3. Optimize Online Profiles

Update LinkedIn, GitHub or portfolio sites with recent projects and achievements.

4. Search & Filter Jobs

Use Jobify.ro’s search, filter by city & category, and save interesting roles.

5. Write Focused Cover Letters

In 3 short paragraphs tell the recruiter why you fit the role and how you’ll add value.

6. Prepare for Interviews

Research the company, rehearse common questions, and have STAR-formatted stories ready.

7. Follow-Up & Track

Politely follow up after interviews and use our “Applications” tab to track status.
