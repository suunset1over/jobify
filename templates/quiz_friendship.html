Quiz: How Friendly Are You at Work?

Take 5 minutes to discover your “office-friend” type and get a fun description of your team vibe.

Instructions

Read each question, choose one answer, then add up your points. (The quiz is purely for fun and self-reflection—no right or wrong!)

1. If your friendship were a movie, which title fits?

  • A. “Just One More Coffee…” — 4 pts
  • B. “Infinite Conversations” — 3 pts
  • C. “Meme Masters” — 2 pts
  • D. “Simply Grateful” — 1 pt

2. How often do you DM non-work content to your colleague?

  • A. Daily—memes, videos, horoscopes! — 4 pts
  • B. Every great meme goes straight to them — 3 pts
  • C. Only if it really resonates — 2 pts
  • D. Rarely, but always on point — 1 pt

3. Morning all-hands meeting—what’s your mood?

  • A. Pumped! They saved me a seat — 4 pts
  • B. Positive—and we’ll debrief right after — 3 pts
  • C. Meh, I’ll sit quietly in the back — 2 pts
  • D. Can I join online instead? — 1 pt

4. Company party: “Movie/M cartoon character” theme. Your costume?

  • A. Iconic duo—full vintage parody — 4 pts
  • B. Recently discussed indie-film brothers — 3 pts
  • C. Whole team dresses prom-style, like a US sitcom — 2 pts
  • D. Solo as my latest binge-series hero — 1 pt

5. Which statement would you love to hear?

  • A. “No TikTok from you—day wasted!” — 4 pts
  • B. “Office isn’t the same without you” — 3 pts
  • C. “I feel there’s a real human beside me” — 2 pts
  • D. “You always know the right words” — 1 pt

Results

  • 6–10 pts – Quiet Backbone
    You offer calm, steady support. The team knows you’ve got their backs.
  • 11–15 pts – Soulful Conversationalist
    Warm and sociable; coffee breaks with you feel like mini therapy.
  • 16–20 pts – Charisma Champion
    You light up the office—energy and jokes flow wherever you go.
  • 21–24 pts – Meme Powerhouse
    A living legend! Your memes are corporate lore and morale fuel.
